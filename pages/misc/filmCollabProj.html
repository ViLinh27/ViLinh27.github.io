Chili Collaboration Project

Helping in a film pitch

An old classmate from undergrad was asking for help with pitching a film concept to pitch to the higher ups at her school in China. I was one of the people that helped with concept art and layouts. I look forward to seeing how the pitch plays out. Updates have been difficult due to recent times. Below are some of the pieces I worked on.

The original concept involved a food themed town so I tried adding as many food themed buildings to a metropolitan style city as I could.

Here we have the main character reacting to her future work place. This was part of the original concept.

Here is the industrial kitchen the main character would work at. I started modeling a rudimentary 3D model of a kitchen to help with modeling and rendered in photoshop.
