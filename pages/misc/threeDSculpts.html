Digital Sculpting

Sculpting in the digital space

I was unaware of digital sculpting as a medium in 3d animation and modeling before taking a class in undergrad. Sculpting was never my strong suit but it was an interesting learning process that I will keep with me through my artistic exploration.

This skull project was meant to get our feet wet after learning the ropes of the Zbrush software. One fundamental we had to learn was human anatomy so the skull was the start of that learning process.

The torso project was a deeper dive in sculpting human anatomy. I chose the male torso as a bigger challenge to myself because it's not the most familiar territory for me.

Other sculpting projects can seen on the sculpting website I had to make for this class here .
